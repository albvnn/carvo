About Carvo

Our Project

Carvo is a student project developed for the TI402I 2024/2025 Web Programming course. Our platform aims to revolutionize how car rental businesses manage their fleets by integrating sustainability metrics with everyday operations.

We believe that the future of transportation is not just about moving people from point A to point B, but doing so in a way that minimizes environmental impact. Carvo provides car rental businesses with the tools to track, analyze, and optimize their carbon footprint while improving operational efficiency.

Sustainability Mission

At Carvo, we're committed to creating digital solutions that align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Our platform primarily supports the following goals:

Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

By helping car rental companies optimize their fleet usage and reduce carbon emissions, we contribute to making cities more sustainable and reducing their environmental impact.

Goal 13: Climate Action

Our analytics tools help businesses measure their carbon footprint and take informed decisions to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from their operations.
